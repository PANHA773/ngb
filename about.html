About ScholarshipPortal

We're on a mission to make global education accessible to everyone by connecting students with life-changing scholarship opportunities.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To democratize access to international education by providing a comprehensive platform that connects students with scholarship opportunities worldwide.

Our Vision

A world where every qualified student can pursue their educational dreams regardless of their financial background or geographic location.

Our Promise

To provide accurate, up-to-date scholarship information and personalized guidance to help students navigate the complex application process.

Our Story

ScholarshipPortal was founded in 2010 by a group of international students who experienced firsthand the challenges of finding and applying for scholarships.

Frustrated by the fragmented information and complex processes, we set out to create a centralized platform that would simplify the search for educational funding opportunities.

What started as a small database of scholarships has grown into a comprehensive platform serving over 2 million students annually from 180+ countries.

Today, we continue to expand our services while staying true to our original mission: making global education accessible to all.
