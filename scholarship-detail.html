Chevening Scholarship UK

Fully-funded master's degree program for future leaders

Master's Degree Fully Funded Deadline: Nov 30, 2025
Scholarship Details

Chevening is the UK government's international awards programme aimed at developing global leaders. Funded by the Foreign, Commonwealth and Development Office and partner organisations, Chevening offers full financial support for future leaders to study for any eligible master's degree at any UK university.

Level of Study

Master's Degree

Fields of Study

All fields available at UK universities

Scholarship Value

Full tuition fees, living allowance, travel costs, and other grants

Eligible Nationalities

Citizens of Chevening-eligible countries (over 160 countries)

Eligibility Criteria
  • Have an undergraduate degree that will enable you to gain entry to a postgraduate program at a UK university
  • Have at least two years of work experience
  • Apply to three different eligible UK university courses and have received an unconditional offer from one of these choices
  • Meet the English language requirement
  • Return to your country of citizenship for a minimum of two years after your award has ended

  2. Prepare application documents (CV, references, etc.)
